Hi, I am Aarav, a 9th grader at Dublin High School. Throughout highschool, students are often nudged towards making decisions that help them reach the career of their choice. I, like others, am no exception, resulting in me choosing classes such as CSE and higher-level math courses, catering towards the direction of a computer science major. Computer science as a subject struck my interest at a level unlike other subjects, leading me to dive deeper into the intricacies of programming and do courses on languages such as Python, C, C++, etc… Though challenging, CS has allowed me to utilize my mentality and thought process to the fullest potential, which is one of the main reasons for me pursuing it in college.

As I have figured out throughout my academic career, I work best when tackling problems from an analytical standpoint, explaining my proficiency at math, my passion for chess, and most importantly, my interest in CS. In contrast to other subjects, CS requires a certain way of thinking, an intricate web of planning, learning, and organization that goes hand-in-hand with my natural thought process. In my eyes, a CS assignment can be comparable to a complex math problem. I start by analyzing the problem, planning out possible ways to solve it. After the initial analysis, one must learn the most efficient way to solve the problem whilst simultaneously organizing the work. My ability to naturally solve problems in this methodical way directly correlates to my ability to be proficient in future AP computer science classes along with CS as a career choice. This way of thinking explains my ability to comprehend concepts quickly, resulting in me completing esteemed courses such as CS50 and many others.

A trait that could be just as or potentially more important than my mentality, however, would be my ability to work hard along with my determination. Although thought processes are job or task specific, a hard-working mentality is required in any field of study in order to be proficient. I have proven myself many times that I can work for hours on end, whether it be a grueling CS50 course, or practicing basketball everyday. My ability to persevere through tiredness and lack of motivation allow me to complete any task I’m given, for example, finishing 4 hours of grueling homework after a tiring basketball practice. This is a situation that I had been in many times before, proving that little obstructions do not stop me from working to my full potential.

In conclusion, I strongly believe that CS is the best subject for me to pursue in high school and further learning. My methodical way of thinking provides an advantage to understand CS concepts easier, helping me become more proficient with every lesson. Moreover, I am a highly motivated and determined worker, working my best to any assignment assigned to me, no matter the situation. Thank you for reading what I have to say, and feel free to explore the rest of what I have to offer on my website.
